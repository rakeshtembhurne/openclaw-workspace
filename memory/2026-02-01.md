## Learning Points

### Technical
- ASP.NET WebForms require browser automation (not just HTTP requests)
- Vector databases enable RAG (retrieval + generation)
- Fine-tuning requires GPU/Cloud compute (can't just use CPU)
- Marathi text encoding needs Unicode handling
- Indian legal data may have access restrictions

### Business
- Solo founders making $M ARR by focusing on ONE product
- B2B SaaS > B2C for AI tools (companies pay)
- Information arbitrage (filtering AI noise) is sustainable business
- Free tier + upgrade path is winning SaaS strategy
- Content at scale (AI-assisted) beats manual creation
- Specialized tools (legal, healthcare) command premium prices

### Strategy
- Validate with small wins before scaling
- Speed to market beats perfection
- Build in public to attract early adopters and build trust
- Use automation for repetitive tasks (content, outreach, research)
- Multiple monetization streams > single revenue source
- Focusing on ONE industry extremely well is better than being average in many
